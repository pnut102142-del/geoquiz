Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

GeoQuiz App ("we", "our", or "us") is a free geography quiz game available at geoquizapp.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and use information when you play.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide leaderboard and multiplayer features:

  • Display name — the name you choose when you first visit (stored locally on your device)
  • Game scores — your quiz results submitted to our leaderboard
  • Visit logs — approximate visit time and which game mode you played (no IP address or personal identifiers)

How We Use Your Information

  • To display your score on leaderboards
  • To track total visitor counts and popular game modes
  • To improve the game experience

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Firebase (Google)

We use Firebase Realtime Database by Google to store leaderboard scores and visit counts. Google may process this data in accordance with their Privacy Policy.

Google AdSense

We display ads served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Cookies & Local Storage

We use your browser's local storage to save:

  • Your chosen display name
  • Your theme preference (dark/light mode)
  • Whether you have visited before

No sensitive personal data is stored in local storage. You can clear this at any time through your browser settings.

Third-Party Map Services

Map tiles are provided by CARTO and Leaflet. Country flags are served from flagcdn.com. These services may log requests in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

GeoQuiz App is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The only "personal" data we collect is a display name chosen by the user.

Data Retention

Leaderboard scores are automatically deleted after 3 days. Visit logs may be retained for up to 30 days for analytics purposes.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect accounts or email addresses, there is no personal profile to delete. You can clear your local display name at any time by clicking your name on the home screen. If you have concerns about data stored in our leaderboard, contact us at the email below.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: numworks.contact@gmail.com

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
